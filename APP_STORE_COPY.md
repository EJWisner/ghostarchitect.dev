## Description (4000 char max)

```
GHOST ARCHITECT MOBILE

The mobile companion to Ghost Architect™ — the AI-powered code triage platform used by engineers and agencies to audit codebases, categorize risk, and prioritize remediation.

Pair Ghost Mobile with Ghost Architect CLI (free on npm) to carry your entire findings portfolio wherever you go.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
WHO IT'S FOR
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• Senior engineers tracking remediation progress
• CTOs presenting audit findings to stakeholders
• Fractional CTOs reviewing multiple client codebases
• Agency leads reporting on client engagements
• Anyone who needs findings context in a meeting

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
WHAT YOU GET
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PORTFOLIO VIEW
See every project you've scanned. Critical findings, remediation percentage, and estimated remaining hours at a glance.

PROJECT DETAIL
Drill into any codebase. View findings by severity, track baseline vs. current state, see scan history, and measure progress over time.

FINDING CARDS
Tap any finding to see the full description, affected files, effort estimate, and cost projection. Everything you need to have an informed conversation — no laptop required.

PRESENT MODE
Optimized view for showing findings to a client or a board. Big readable cards, remediation metrics front and center, no developer cruft.

SHARE REPORTS
Export any project summary to share with your team or leadership.

BASELINE TRACKING
Set a baseline scan, then measure every future scan against it. See what's been fixed, what's still open, and what's new since last time.

TRY THE DEMO
Not ready to connect your own data yet? Tap "See a live demo" on first launch. You'll get a real portfolio — 18 findings scanned from a public Magento extension — showing exactly how remediation tracking works across multiple scans.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
HOW IT CONNECTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Ghost Mobile reads your scan results from a private GitHub repository you control. Scans are generated by Ghost Architect CLI and published to your ghost-reports repo. The mobile app reads that repo — nothing is uploaded to our servers.

Your GitHub personal access token is stored in the device's hardware-backed keychain. Never in plain text. Never transmitted except to GitHub.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PRIVACY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• Your code never touches our servers
• Your scan results live in your own private GitHub repo
• Your PAT stays on your device
• Zero analytics, zero telemetry, zero tracking

Learn more at ghostarchitect.dev
Ghost Architect CLI: github.com/EJWisner/ghost-architect-open
```

## App Privacy Questionnaire — Answers

Apple's structured form in App Store Connect → App Privacy section.

### Data Collection
**Does your app collect any data?** → **NO**

Ghost Mobile reads scan results from the user's own private GitHub repo. The app does not send any data to Ghost Platform servers. The GitHub PAT is stored in the device keychain only. No analytics, telemetry, or tracking.
