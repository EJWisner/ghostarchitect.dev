01 · STACK REALITY

What's there.

An independent inventory of every framework, runtime, and dependency actually running in production. No reliance on what the seller said in the pitch deck.

  • Framework and runtime detection across any language
  • EOL flags with publication dates
  • Version drift across services
  • Verified against actual repo contents, not docs
02 · KEY-PERSON RISK

Who wrote it.

Bus factor math from anonymized git authorship. Identifies the people whose departure would meaningfully impact your ability to operate or modify the codebase.

  • Anonymized authorship analysis
  • Bus factor calculation
  • Departed-contributor flagging
  • Concentration risk per subsystem
03 · HIDDEN DEPENDENCIES

What you owe.

The license-and-EOL map you should have asked for before signing. Surfaces copyleft licenses in commercial code, deprecated libraries, and dependencies that hit EOL months or years ago.

  • License classification across the dep tree
  • Copyleft exposure in commercial code
  • EOL dependency flagging
  • Transitive dependency risk
04 · MODERNIZATION ROADMAP

What to do.

A confidence-rated, AI-synthesized 90-day plan. Stabilize or rebuild recommendation, prioritized by deal impact. Source-grounded, with traceable evidence.

  • Stabilize-or-rebuild recommendation
  • Confidence rating (LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH)
  • 90-day plan with deal-tier prioritization
  • Source-grounded; no hallucinated findings
